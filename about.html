FAQs

FAQs

How long does detailing take?

Timing is dependant on condition & size of your ride as well as the package of choice. Feel free to contact us for more accurate timing information.

What are the advantages of Paint Protection & how long does it last?

At Radiant Rides Auto Care your ride goes through our entire decontamination & polish process to ensure that when we protect or seal your car, the paint is in its best condition allowing the sealant (Ceramic/Graphene Coating) to bond better to the paint. Longevity is dependent on what product you choose, varying from 3, 5, or 8 years, as well as how you maintain your vehicle, if it's garage kept and how often it is exposed to the elements.

Are you mobile?

We are not a mobile detailing service. All general detailing services are carried out at our Cranbourne North location. However, we do offer mobile headlight restoration services, which are available by appointment and include a call-out fee.
